Mensen Verhalen

Exposition & Storytelling

April 2023

'Mensen Verhalen' is something completely different; It's an prototype exhibition i set up together with Lanxiang Vermeulen & Vivanne Hoogendam.

The preparations

Bringing people together through books: That was the idea that set everything in motion. With Lanxiang's passion for photography, Vivanne's love for books, and my dedication to stage design and storytelling, we successfully created this mini/prototype exhibition.

The plan

In addition to interviewing 50 people on the street about their reading habits and favorite books, we highlighted the stories of 5 individuals who truly stood out. These 5 people, representing different generations, all shared a love for reading. Each person was given their own "room," designed to reflect their story, creating a unique and personal world for visitors to experience. After designing all the rooms, the last 4 days were a whirlwind of cutting, pasting, and assembling. It was a period of round-the-clock effort, but in the final seconds, it all came together: a prototype that we were genuinely proud of.

Reception

We never expected that the three of us could create something so impressive in just two and a half weeks. The feedback was overwhelmingly positive. The stories left by visitors on the wall at "Story Square," where everyone could share their own stories , were a joy to read. Thanks to this project, we also received the Audience Award at the project market.

The Exposition

Below, you can watch our video showcasing the entire exhibition. We are delighted to announce the successful completion of this project, earning us a well-deserved grade of 10!
