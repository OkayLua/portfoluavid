Club Safe

Brand/Identity, Web Design & Content

November 2023

Club Safe is one of my favourite projects ever. It is a Non-Profit organisation that helps students and outher young adults clubbing safely. Keypoints in this project were content, tone of voice and branding.

The challenge

The assignment was as follows:

As a duo, you will collaboratively conceive a business. The business should 'sell' at least two things: itself and a service or product. Choose a topic, product, or service that you have an affinity for. You are forming a small enterprise, sole proprietorship, or start-up. Your objective is to design content for your company's website that aligns with the business goals, the users (target audience), user goals, and user queries.

In General

ClubSafe is dedicated to improving the safety of students and young adults in clubbing environments. Through a comprehensive approach, the organization promotes responsible behavior, implements safety protocols, and provides resources for informed decision-making. By addressing the unique challenges of this demographic, ClubSafe plays a crucial role in fostering a secure and enjoyable nightlife experience.

ClubSafe is a Non-profit organisation funded by the Dutch Ministry of Health, Welfare & Sport. In addition to a dedicated core team comprising dozens of individuals, numerous volunteers contribute daily to enhance the safety of the nightlife, making it a better experience for everyone.

What we Offer

ClubSafe engages in three primary activities:

Guides

ClubSafe provides guides to prepare users for any nightlife situation, whether it's their first time or hundredth, offering valuable insights and tips for a confident and enjoyable experience.

Club Lists

Discover clubs we've thoroughly researched and evaluated, focusing on crucial aspects like safety and accessibility!Our detailed assessments aim to provide you with comprehensive insights, ensuring you make informed choices for a secure and enjoyable nightlife experience.

Presentations

Presentations are primarily conducted at universities and come in various forms and sizes. Collaborating with our team involves exploring ways to make them as personalized and unique as possible.

Brand Identity

ClubSafe embodies accessibility: On this website, everyone should feel at home and secure. Our core objectives in tone of voice, content, and design are as follows

approachable

We ensure the use of our service is accessible with engaging titles, photos, and snacks to create a welcoming environment.

Light-Hearted

While addressing serious topics, we maintain a light-hearted approach, making the content enjoyable for everyone and avoiding intimidation.

Open/Supportive

Balancing a light yet serious tone, we present our service as open and trustworthy, fostering a sense of safety and support for the user.
